Heartstopper Vol 1
Alice Oseman
Genres: Graphic Novel, Romance, LGBTQ+
Hachette - Hodder Children's Group
Age Range:
     Content: 12+
     Reading Comprehension Level: 12+

Boy meets boy. Boys become friends. Boys fall in love. A bestselling LGBTQ+ graphic novel about life, love, and everything that happens in between.

Charlie and Nick are at the same school, but they've never met ... until one day when they're made to sit together. They quickly become friends, and soon Charlie is falling hard for Nick, even though he doesn't think he has a chance.

But love works in surprising ways, and Nick is more interested in Charlie than either of them realised.
